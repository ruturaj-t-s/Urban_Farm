Pricing FAQs

Common questions about our plans and pricing.

Can I change plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. We'll help you transition your system and adjust billing accordingly.

What happens if I cancel?

You can cancel anytime. You'll keep your system and can continue growing with purchased seed pods. We also offer a buy-out option for the hardware.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees! The monthly price includes your system, seed pods, support, and all software features. Only additional costs are optional accessories.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes! We offer student discounts, bulk pricing for businesses, and seasonal promotions. Contact our sales team for current offers.
