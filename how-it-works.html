What to Expect

Track your plants' journey from seed to harvest with our detailed timeline.

1

Day 1-3: Germination

Seeds are planted in our specially designed pods. The system provides optimal temperature and humidity for quick germination.

2

Week 1-2: Seedling Stage

First leaves appear! The system adjusts light intensity and nutrients to support rapid growth during this critical phase.

3

Week 2-4: Vegetative Growth

Plants develop their full structure. You'll see significant growth daily as the system optimizes conditions for each plant.

4

Week 4+: Harvest Time

Time to enjoy! Harvest fresh produce as needed. Many plants will continue producing for weeks with proper care.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our urban farming systems.

How much maintenance is required?

Minimal! Our systems are designed for busy lifestyles. You'll need to refill water every 1-2 weeks and replace nutrient pods monthly. The system handles everything else automatically.

What can I grow?

Leafy greens (lettuce, spinach, kale), herbs (basil, mint, cilantro), microgreens, and small fruiting plants like cherry tomatoes. We provide seed pods optimized for each plant type.

How much energy does it use?

Our LED systems are highly efficient, using about as much energy as a standard light bulb. Most systems cost less than $10 per month in electricity.

Do I need any gardening experience?

Not at all! Our systems are designed for complete beginners. The app guides you through every step, and our support team is always available to help.
